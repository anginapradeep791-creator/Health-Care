Basic First Aid Tips

Heart Attack

Call emergency services immediately and keep the patient calm until help arrives.

Bleeding

Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth and seek medical attention immediately.

Burns

Cool the affected area with clean running water. Do not apply ice directly.
